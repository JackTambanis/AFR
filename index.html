About Me

I’m Jack, 12 years old, and a front-end developer and app creator. From an early age, I’ve been curious about how digital things work. While other kids used apps, I explored how systems and code operate behind the scenes. I’ve taught myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Lua, and C++, starting with simple game scripts and moving on to small coding projects, creating interfaces, and automating tasks. I’m also interested in hardware and electronics. I’ve spent time working with Arduino and ESP32 to build projects and experiment with automation. Recently, I completed a Front-End Development Certification. I also help plan and run creative events with the Bayside Boombox Event Committee in my community.
